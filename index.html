About Us

We all have to eat, and Chef Chris Clarke is just the food dork to chat with you about it. Diverse guests share how food impacted them, and the tasty, memorable and outright ridiculous things they’ve eaten. It isn’t just food, it’s people, their connections to what they eat and us. In true Global Citizen fashion, he then started a foundation, Food for Soul, to expand the concept to cities around the world, like Rio de Janeiro and London. He’s currently in the process of bringing two Refettorios to the US, the Guardian reports.
